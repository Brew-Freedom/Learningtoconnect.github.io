title: "Why This Site Exists"
---

I’ve always found communication harder than it seems for other people.

Not just starting conversations—but keeping them going. 
Not just meeting people—but maintaining friendships over time.

For a long time, I thought this was just something I was bad at.

This site isn’t about pretending to have everything figured out.

It’s about:
- figuring things out slowly
- sharing what actually works (and what doesn’t)
- helping other people who feel the same way

If you’ve ever:
- not known what to say
- overthought a simple message
- drifted from people without meaning to

You’re not alone.

This is a place to learn how to connect—step by step.
