Sometimes friendships fade not because of big conflicts, but because of small things:

- Not replying for a day or two  
- Forgetting to check in  
- Feeling awkward about reaching out  

A small message like “Hey, how’s your week going?” can make a difference.  
This site is full of small, practical ways to maintain connections.
